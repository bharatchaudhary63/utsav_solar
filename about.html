About UTSAV TECHNO ENTERPRISE

UTSAV TECHNO ENTERPRISE (Techno Enterprise) is a solar energy company based in Aakhol, Deesa. We design, supply, install, and maintain solar power systems for homes, businesses, and institutions. Our mission is to provide cost-effective, efficient, and sustainable solar solutions to the people in Deesa and nearby regions.

We believe in quality workmanship, using the best components, and providing excellent after-sales service. Whether you're looking for a small residential rooftop setup or a large solar farm, we have the expertise and commitment to deliver.
